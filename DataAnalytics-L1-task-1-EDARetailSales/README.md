# Task 1 – Retail Sales Data Analysis (EDA)

## Objective
Perform exploratory data analysis (EDA) on the retail sales dataset to uncover patterns in customer demographics, product categories, and sales trends. The goal is to generate actionable insights for business decision-making.

## Dataset
- **File:** `retail_sales_dataset.csv`
- **Columns:**
  - Transaction ID
  - Date
  - Customer ID
  - Gender
  - Age
  - Product Category
  - Quantity
  - Price per Unit
  - Total Amount
- **Source:** Self-provided dataset for internship task

## Tech Stack
Python, pandas, numpy, matplotlib, seaborn, Jupyter Notebook

## Workflow
1. Load dataset and inspect structure
2. Handle missing values and inconsistent data
3. Generate descriptive statistics (mean, median, mode, distributions)
4. Visualise sales trends:
   - Sales by product category
   - Sales by gender and age group
   - Monthly/weekly sales trends
5. Identify top customers and high-value product categories
6. Summarise key insights

## Outputs
- Bar charts: product category sales
- Pie chart: gender distribution
- Line chart: sales trends over time
- Histogram: age distribution of customers
- Top 10 customers by total spend

## Insights
- Which product categories drive the most revenue
- Demographic breakdown of customers
- Seasonal or monthly sales patterns
- Identification of VIP customers
